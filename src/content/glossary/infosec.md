---
name: Information Security (InfoSec)
description: The practice of protecting information and systems from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, modification, or destruction.
---

Information Security, often shortened to InfoSec, focuses on keeping information and systems safe from unauthorized access or misuse.

It encompasses policies, technical safeguards, training, and incident response. Common objectives are often summarized as confidentiality, integrity, and availability.

A strong InfoSec program provides the foundation for many compliance efforts by ensuring that security practices are consistent and well-documented.
