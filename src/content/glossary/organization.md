---
name: Organization
description: A top-level grouping representing a company, customer, or similar entity within a system.
---

An organization is a top-level container that represents a company or similar entity within a product.

Organizations typically own users, projects, configurations, and billing relationships. In multi-tenant SaaS products, each customer often corresponds to one or more organizations.

Understanding how organizations are modeled is crucial for scoping access controls, compliance obligations, and audit evidence.
