name: American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA)
description: The professional body that defines SOC reporting standards and criteria and accredits firms that perform SOC audits.
---

The American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA) is the organization that sets standards for SOC reporting, including SOC 1, SOC 2, and SOC 3.

For SOC 2 specifically, the AICPA publishes the Trust Services Criteria and the guidance CPAs use to plan and perform audits. Only licensed CPA firms operating under AICPA rules can issue official SOC reports.
