---
name: System Components
description: The individual elements that together form a system, such as infrastructure, software, people, processes, and data.
---

System components are the building blocks that make up a system: infrastructure, software, people, processes, and data.

Understanding components and how they interact helps teams design controls, monitor system health, and explain how the system supports business objectives.

Breaking systems down into components is especially useful for complex architectures and during incident investigations.
